PhonoPlay

Your Music, Your Way

What is this?

A browser-based music player that allows users to upload and play songs locally with playlist support and advanced playback controls.

Why it was built

This project was created as a learning exercise to understand web development basics including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Features

  • Upload songs from device
  • Playlist management
  • Shuffle playback
  • Loop mode
  • Progress tracking
  • All normal functionality of the music player
